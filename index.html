This is a repository containing the code associated with my Master Thesis. This homepage contains some animations showing some of the results.

Single Lane Network

The video below shows the results for the single lane network. The blue square represents the bus with non-optimal control parameters, and the green square represents the bus with optimal control parameters. The amount of traffic is represented by a red color scale. White means there is no traffic, and full ref means the road is congested. The density on the road is the density from the simulation with optimal control parameters.

Two-to-two Junction Network

The video below shows the results for the two-to-two junction network. The two blue squares represents the buses with non-optimal control parameters, and the green squares represents the buses with optimal control parameters. The amount of traffic is represented by a red color scale. White means there is no traffic, and full ref means the road is congested. The density on the road is the density from the simulation with optimal control parameters. In this network we assume that all of the traffic from the left road enters the right road, and that all of the traffic from the top road enters the bottom road. The junction is controlled by a traffic light.
